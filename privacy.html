Privacy Policy

Effective date: 8 February 2026

The Capybara Squad App respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, use, or share any personal or non-personal data from users directly.

Information Collection

The Capybara Squad App does not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Usage data
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Analytics or tracking data

Third-Party Services

The Capybara Squad App uses Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. If you choose to sign in to Game Center, Apple may process data such as your Game Center profile, scores, and achievements according to Apple's own terms and privacy policy. See Apple's Privacy Policy .

Data Security

The app does not directly store or process user data. Any data handled through Apple Game Center is managed by Apple.

Children's Privacy

The Capybara Squad App does not collect any information from users of any age, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be made available within the app or on its distribution page.
